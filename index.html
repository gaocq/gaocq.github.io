Biography

Currently, I am served as an Assistant Researcher of Medical School, and the Assistant Dean of National Institute of Healthcare Data Science at Nanjing University, leading by Prof. Junfeng Zhang.
I visited IDEA Lab at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, under the supervision of Prof. Dinggang Shen, funded by CSC.
I received my Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University, under the supervision of Prof. Yang Gao.
